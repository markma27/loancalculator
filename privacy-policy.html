Privacy Policy

Dark Mode

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Loan Calculator handles your information. Our calculator is designed to operate entirely within your browser, ensuring maximum privacy and security for your financial calculations.

Information Collection

Our loan calculator:

  • Does not collect or store any personal information
  • Performs all calculations locally in your browser
  • Does not transmit your financial data to any servers
  • Only stores your dark mode preference in your browser's local storage

Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage only to remember your display preferences (dark/light mode). This information never leaves your device and is not used for tracking or analytics purposes.

Data Security

Since all calculations are performed locally in your browser:

  • Your financial data never leaves your device
  • No information is stored on external servers
  • Your calculations are completely private

Downloads

When you download calculation results (Excel files):

  • Files are generated locally in your browser
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • Downloads are saved directly to your device

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
